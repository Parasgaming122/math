How to Use Past Papers Effectively

  1. Start with topic papers — practice one topic at a time until you're confident.
  2. Time yourself — P1 is 75 minutes. Do full papers under timed conditions once you've covered all topics.
  3. Read the mark scheme carefully — understand HOW marks are awarded (M = method, A = accuracy, B = independent).
  4. Review examiner reports — they tell you common mistakes students made. Don't make them too!
  5. Redo questions you got wrong — don't just read the answer. Actually redo the working.
  6. Track weak areas — note which topics you lose marks on, then revise those specifically.

Exam Timetable Information

Paper 1 (Pure Mathematics 1) is typically examined in:
• May/June session (main session for most candidates)
• October/November session
• February/March session (limited regions)

Duration: 1 hour 45 minutes (105 minutes)
Total marks: 75 marks
Calculator: Scientific calculator allowed (not graphical)

Check the official CIE timetable at cambridgeinternational.org for exact dates.
